Bio

I am a front-end web developer with experience in creative design and editing. I attended the front-end web development bootcamp at Grand Circus Detroit, an intensive 8-week course that focused on learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I earned my bachelors degree in public relations from Western Michigan University where I graduated cum laude.

My interest in coding started at a young age, watching my uncle program our family's computer. That lead to dabbling in coding in high school with my own web page. While I attended college for communications, and later worked as an administrative assistant at a non-profit organization, my interest in coding and passion for creating reemerged. I look forward to putting my new skills to work!

When not busy coding and designing, I enjoy spending time at home with my husband in Farmington Hills. I also enjoy traveling, playing pub trivia, attending aerial arts classes, and playing French horn in the Farmington Community Band.
